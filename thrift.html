Thriftn

Buy. Sell. Reuse. Sustain.

Thriftn is an e-commerce platform in Pakistan that promotes buying and selling second-hand items. Our mission is to provide affordable second-hand items and promote environmental sustainability.

Key Features:

  • Smart technology ensuring quality items
  • High-quality image uploads
  • AI-based recommendation system
  • Trusted seller badges
  • Direct negotiation with sellers
  • 24/7 chatbot support
  • Option to donate sale proceeds to NGOs
  • Delivery via Trax.pk

Environmental Impact:

  • Reducing waste by reusing items
  • Lowering carbon footprint through sustainable shopping
  • Encouraging a circular economy

Target Audience: All age groups
